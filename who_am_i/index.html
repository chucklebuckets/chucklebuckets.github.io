hey BUDDY!!

I am Charlie and Charlie is I. I'll occasionally humor such names as Charl, Chazburger, Charfunkle, Chucklebucket, Charnely, Charles Lee, C-Buddy, C-Dawg, Chimp, Chump, Chizzle, Chag, Chester, Buddythwomp, the Big Do, Chuck, Chaz, Chez, Chiz, Charleston, Professor Albuquerque, Chuckleberry, Bizmo-Buddy, Prunklehead, Charlemagne Cheddarfist, Char-Char, and Carlos, but I'd prefer if you'd hold the fanfare. I was born in Sarasota, Florida, though I've spent the majority of my life dotting around Indiana. I am freshly 18 and done with high school. I do not mesh with the path that has been decided for me as an American youth and find it cathartic to know I have some level of autonomy over it now. I am incredibly fortunate in many ways.

I choose to base my life around production over consumption. If you want to see what I make check out my PAST GLORIES. I like to throw my hours at "the arts" but most of my time lands in front of the computer. Game development is not a passion but a lifeline. A prophetic endeavor that has had hold of me from the ripe old age of four. I remember it like yesterMay when my younger'nt brother said "You know, people MAKE the games we play." I just never would've thought...

Production over consumption but still I consume; I'm picky of my input. Time is sacred to me and I can't bear to waste it with infinite mind-prism algorithmically synthesized slop. I avoid short-form content but I'm a sucker for YouTube videos. I'd offer a genre but my enjoyment seems solely bound to the creator. Take a peek at my bracketed list of people I'll give watch to. Joel Haver stands out as one of the most genuine.

Watching crap is great but boy do I love playing video games. I'll try anything but I hold my love close. See all my favorite james here, though I am inclined to include word of such masterpieces as Noita, Worms Armageddon, and Vidiot Game before you do.

My ears still work and every-buddy with working ears loves music. Sniff around in this big dump of artists, albums, and songs I tune into. King Gizzard and the Lizard Wizard and KMD are probably the two artists that rent my ears most often. Albuquerque isn’t a song but a lyfestyle.

Maybe I'm in the mood to watch stuff again--maybe I'm talking about shows, movies, cartoons and the works. I enjoy an oo, maybe an ah. I enjoy looking at little things on screen zip around with bright colors and big movements. See my fav movies here, cartoons here. I should mention I am not able to get over Napoleon Dynamite nor Xavier Renegade Angel after multiple watches and multiple years--the shortest path to the deepest ventricle of my chest-pounder is by way of funny bone. Freak anatomy aside, I enjoy a good laugh above all else, regardless of medium.

Sometimes I watch things in my mind too: All Tomorrows and The Wonderling stand out as my two favorite books, and I am a fan of the Lord of the Rings series.

I enjoy rock climbing. I have climbed exclusively indoors with hopes to touch real rock soon. Camping, backpacking, and caving have all been fascinating ideas to me but have not been actionable yet.

I play the drums. I have never been in a band nor had any professional lessons. When I am not frustrated by my lack of chops I am recording little ditties I come up with using my beautiful Scarlett Focusrite.

I dream of nomadism; I refuse security. Life is best against the grain; I would sooner choose to live in the sewer than the city.

I abstain from substances; "using socially" is for those with friends less funny than my own. I subsequently abstain from poison.

I've used Obsidian for a good chunk of time now and always had the desire to write to eyes other than my own; if you want more of my words check out WHIMS to see what I've written up. If you would like to finish the conversation I have started you can contact me at SEND WORD, where you can find my buddypals and externals as well. This website is one big self indulgent swamp of Charlie from tip to taint--if this page has not exhausted you there are plenty more that will.

If you fear I have done a poor job introducing myself or hold a deep contempt for me you can behead me here.
