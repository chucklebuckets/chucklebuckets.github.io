INITIAL FORAY

Development on this'n webbysite was steady for a good little week or two there, but I haven't done squat in these past few days. I hit a pretty hard wall in trying to write a blog post worthy of being the very first. It's dawning on me now that nothing even matters cuz I can just delete or edit the damn thing whenever I want to anyhow. It's MY show! On with it:

I’m prone to feeling uncomfortable writing to eyes that aren’t my own. It’s very easy to look at things I wrote 1 year ago or 10 seconds ago and think they read with a sense of entitlement, awkwardness, or think they’re just plain ol’ cringe. Welp, I figure Occam's Razor is to avoid this conundrummer’s thought-ocost by laying it bare and letting the sun penetrate deep. I don't want to bother polishing these turds into diamonds. This blog is coal to me. I don’t intend to write with utmost grammatical correctness nor with respect to sentence structure. More than anything with these WHIMS, I'd like to step away from the shackles of perfectionism and just start writing crud. I want something more raw; this whole thing is for me more than anyone else anyways, at least as it stands now.

I have a few would-be initial blog posts that are half written. Arbitrary subjects whose purposes were to fade this confrontation. Maybe I'll get around to finishing them, maybe I'll just scrap 'em. Regardless, it just feels G-Dang nice to get comfortable. Like a hermit crab finding a shell. Once so naked; no longer.
